About Retain

A platform to help users memorise statements.

Created with extension support in mind, this is a project that I worked on for my final undergraduate year to help me get the hang of technologies surrouding web development. As a fan of spaced repetition learning but as someone who is generally lazy, I decided to build a platform that allows for forcing the user to stick to revising what they learn and this is the outcome.

Full source code can be found on GitHub.

The Process

Choose a Collection

Choose from user created collections or create your own.

Learn Statements

Activate a collection and begin learning its contents in sessions.

Review Statements

Keep reviewing what you have already learned to retain it.

Grab an Extension

Integrate the reviewing process into your life using one of the extensions provided.

Extensible

Developed with support for extensions in mind to allow for easier integration into the user's life
